## What PhoneCare Does

PhoneCare puts everything in one place. No digging through Settings. No confusion. Just a clean, simple app that shows you what's happening and helps you fix it.

### Six things. One app. No nonsense.

**1. Phone Health Dashboard**  
A single score that tells you how your phone is doing — storage, battery, photos, contacts, and privacy all in one view. Green is good. You'll know instantly if anything needs attention.

**2. Storage Analyzer**  
See exactly where your storage is going. "Photos: 42GB. Messages: 12GB. Apps: 6GB." No estimates. No fake warnings. Just the truth — and a clear path to free up space.

**3. Duplicate & Similar Photo Finder**  
Found 847 photos that look the same? PhoneCare shows them to you in groups so you can keep the best one and delete the rest. And if you change your mind? There's an undo button. Always.

**4. Duplicate Contact Merger**  
Have the same person saved 4 different ways? PhoneCare finds them all, shows you both versions side by side, and merges them safely. You can undo that too.

**5. Battery Health Monitor**  
See how your battery is doing today, and how it's changed over time. No more guessing why it drains so fast. Simple charts. Plain explanations. No alarm bells.

**6. Privacy Audit**  
Find out which apps have access to your camera, microphone, location, and contacts. See it all in one screen. Go straight to Settings to change anything that doesn't feel right.

## Built With Care

PhoneCare is 100% on-device. Your photos, contacts, and data never leave your iPhone. There is no server that stores your information. There are no ads. There are no third-party analytics SDKs. Everything PhoneCare knows about your phone stays on your phone.

This is not a marketing claim. It's the technical architecture. It is physically impossible for PhoneCare to share your data — because we never receive it.

# PhoneCare Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

PhoneCare is designed to work on your iPhone without sending your personal data to our servers.

## What We Collect

PhoneCare does not collect personal data from you.

- No account sign-up data
- No photos uploaded to our servers
- No contacts uploaded to our servers
- No advertising identifiers for tracking
- No third-party analytics SDK user profiling

## How PhoneCare Works

PhoneCare processes cleanup scans and privacy checks on-device.

- Photo and contact analysis happens on your phone
- Scan results are stored locally on your device
- Subscription state is managed through Apple's StoreKit

## Data Sharing

PhoneCare does not sell your data and does not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all PhoneCare app data at any time in the app:

- Open Settings
- Open Your Privacy
- Tap Delete All PhoneCare Data

This removes PhoneCare scan history, preferences, and cached app data from your device.

## Third-Party Services

PhoneCare does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Apple frameworks may be used to provide core app features (for example, Photos, Contacts, and StoreKit) according to Apple's platform rules and your permission settings.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact support through the support details shown in the app's About screen.
